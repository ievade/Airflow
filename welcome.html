Welcome to Airflow!

Airflow is a minimalist operating system that runs entirely in your web browser, so you can unleash the fun, without anything getting in your way!
Airflow is currently in development, so expect some bugs along the way.



The featured apps are right in your taskbar so you can easily access them by moving your mouse to the top of the screen. You can find more apps by going into the Apps menu, by clicking on the Airflow logo at the top left.



Have Fun!
